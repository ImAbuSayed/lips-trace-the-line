[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
A quiet touch in the fading light
I pull you close against my chest
I want to lose myself tonight
And put your every fear to rest

[Verse 1]
The dusty road is fast asleep
The shadows stretch across the floor
I have a promise I will keep
Just standing at your open door

[Pre-Chorus]
There is a fire in your eyes
A sudden burning deep and sweet
A quiet look of sweet surprise
When our bodies finally meet

[Chorus]
Let my lips trace the line
Right along your open skin
Leaving every doubt behind
As this midnight we begin

[Post-Chorus]
(Trace the line, trace the line)
[Whispered] (Leaving every doubt behind)

[Verse 2]
The headlights flicker on the wall
A passing truck from way down south
I lean right in to catch your fall
And taste the honey on your mouth

[Pre-Chorus 2]
There is a fire in your eyes
A sudden burning deep and sweet
A quiet look of sweet surprise
When our bodies finally meet

[Chorus]
Let my lips trace the line
Right along your open skin
Leaving every doubt behind
As this midnight we begin

[Post-Chorus 2]
(Trace the line, trace the line)
[Whispered] (Leaving every doubt behind)

[Bridge]
The shadows wrap around us now
A gentle rhythm in the dark
I kiss the worry from your brow
And gently fan this growing spark
I give you all the love you need
Forever yours, forever Saeed

[Spoken]
(Just close your eyes and let it go)
(I want to map the stars upon your skin)

[Final Chorus]
Let my lips trace the line
Right along your open skin
Leaving every doubt behind
As this midnight we begin
